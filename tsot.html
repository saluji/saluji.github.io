Description

2D top down exploration game.
You are playing as a raccoon named Tanuki, experiencing oppression from the perspective of the oppressed ones.

You can download the game here!

Main Task
  • player controller & animation
  • story, writing & world building
  • world Interaction
  • dialogue system
  • enemy AI
  • HUD, icons, title & game over screen
  • room transition
  • music & sound
Software
